Data Mesh Learning

This website is designed to help those of all knowledge/experience levels with data mesh find useful information and join the growing data mesh community.

We are committed to fostering communities of empathy, sharing, and open feedback. If you have suggestions for additional content, we want to hear it. If you want changes, we want to hear it. Feel free to make suggestions in the Slack channel or directly to Scott Hirleman or email us community@datameshlearning.com.
